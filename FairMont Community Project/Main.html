Who is the Fairmont Community?

The Fairmont Community is a small, urban, unicorporated neighborhood located between Lockport and Joliet in Will County, Illinois. Originally founded in the 1870's, Fairmont has served as a place for generations to call home. The Fairmont-Lewis Coalition brings together citizens, educators and leaders from the Fairmont Community and Lewis University in order to build strategic and sustainable partnerships for responding effectively to community identified priorities.
